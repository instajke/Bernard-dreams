We have two types of markets: Real market and Simulated market. The first type is like... Like real market, u can either sell your currencies here or create offers to buy it with specified price. Simulated markets are a little bit different. The price is specified by market manager (developer) and its exchange rates are influenced by customers' supply and demand. Making offers on simulated markets u r guaranteed to get your in-game-money with the best price.
